Overview

Tobinco Media Group (“TMG,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the TMG mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Non-Registered Users: You can browse news, trending topics, and program information without creating an account. We may collect limited, non-identifiable information such as device type, operating system, and usage statistics.
  • Registered Users (Comments): To post comments, you must create an account. We may collect your name/username, email address, encrypted password, and optional profile details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to provide and improve services, enable commenting, personalize content, detect abuse, and communicate updates. We may also use aggregated or anonymized data for analytics.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share data with service providers who perform services on our behalf, to comply with legal obligations, or to protect rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

5. Your Choices

You may browse without logging in, update or delete your account information, and opt out of non-essential communications. To request deletion of your account and personal data, use the Delete Account page.

6. Children’s Privacy

TMG does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.
